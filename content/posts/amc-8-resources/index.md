# AMC-8 Resources

This page contains helpful resources for preparing for the AMC-8 (American Mathematics Competition for students in grades 8 and below).

## Official Resources

- [MAA AMC 8 Official Page](https://maa.org/math-competitions/amc-8)
- Past AMC-8 problems and solutions

## Practice Materials

### Problem Sets

- Official AMC-8 practice problems
- Similar competition problems (MATHCOUNTS, Math Kangaroo)

### Online Resources

- Art of Problem Solving (AoPS)
- Khan Academy mathematics courses
- Alcumus (AoPS online practice system)

## Study Tips

1. **Practice regularly** - Work on problems consistently
2. **Review solutions** - Learn from mistakes and alternative approaches
3. **Time management** - Practice under timed conditions
4. **Understand concepts** - Don't just memorize formulas

## Topics to Cover

- Number Theory
- Counting and Probability
- Geometry
- Algebra
- Logic and Problem Solving
